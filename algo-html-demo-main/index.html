SolarChain

Welcome to the photovoltaik (PV) investment and power purchase agreement (PPA) matching platform!

Introduction

We provide a new way to invest in renewable energy.

Getting started

Before you begin, you should have the AlgoSigner Google Chrome extension You should have ALGO and USDC in your wallet.

1. Photovoltaik NFT Minting

Admins from SolarChain can create tokens linked to physical assets.

2. Invest in new installations

Investors can buy new PV tokens with USDC stablecoin tokens. They can select the type of installation. coming soon: credit card payment

3. PV token marketplace

Investors can trade their PV tokens with other investors on our platform. With the blockchain they can also transfer their tokens independently from us.

4. PPA marketplace

Consumers and corporates are able to bid for a) supply agreements for Certificates of Origin and b) for power purchase agreements (PPAs). Via smart contract the fee is directly paid to the PV token owners. maintenance fees are automatically deducted
